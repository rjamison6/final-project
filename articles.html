Tech Tips

If you are going to work on your bike, you need ample space. Your shop or garage should be kept neat and clean. When disassembling, make sure you have bags or containers for loose parts as some of these parts are expensive and/or unavailable for purchase. If you do need to replace parts, Bob's BMW® and Max's BMW® have factory parts for sale. Boxer 2 valve has aftermarket stuff that BMW® no longer makes. Check out the tools page for a complete list of the tools you will need. You will need a repair or shop manuel. I use the Clymer for BMW® R50/5 Through R100GS 1970 - 1996. This book contains torque specs, valve tolerances, color electrical schematics, and just about anything else you need.
