Special Projects

A popular customization for old bikes is adding a fairing or panier bags. This is a less expensive option that allows for a great deal of creativity. There are a great deal of aftermarket fairings available for both modern and old bikes.
Sidecars have always been popular on old bikes. If you want a sidecar on your bike, you can either look for a sidecar off a Ural or buy a sidecar kit. Both options are expensive, but with a kit you can add features like modern brakes, leaning platform, or modern suspension.
The most expensive modification you can get is the Cafe Racer look. It entails custom body modifications, custom seat, customization of your engine, transmission, etc. and can run thousands of dollars.
