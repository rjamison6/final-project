Willkommen

This is the place to learn about BMW® motorcycles from 1969 to 1996. They are called "airheads" because they are air cooled without a radiator. With their stylish good looks and their mechanical reliability, these bikes are more desirable today than they were back when they were new. If they are properly maintained, they will run forever. If you were wondering how to own one, how to fix one, or you have always wanted one, this is the site for you. Even if you are just looking, willkommen. You are in the right place. Take your shoes off and make yourself at home. Wir haben zusammen noch keine Schweine gehütet!
