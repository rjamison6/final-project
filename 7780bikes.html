1977-1979 BMW/7

The 1977 model year saw more changes for the BMW® airhead. Now the model lineup included the R75/7, the R100/7, the R100S and the R100RS/RT. The R100S would continue on with the sport fairing made famous by the R90S models. The RS and RT fairing would be BMW®s first forray into full fairings. The R60 and R90 models would be discontinued. In 1978, BMW would also add the R80/7 and R80GS. All models are still equiped with the drum brake rear and single front disc break, except the R100S, which came equipped with dual front brakes. They would also get rid of the kickstarter.
